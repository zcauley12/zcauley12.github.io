Team Remote Controllers


Group Members:

Zachary Cauley, Brian Woolfolk, Tommy Lee, Angela Tsao, and Hyung Ryu


Synopsis: Hands Up! Speak Up!


For students and workers who meet up virtually via Zoom or Microsoft Teams, problems could arise when multiple people start to talk at the same time.

After looking at existing solutions and the principles of the current solution, we were able to propose a feature that could resolve our issue.
